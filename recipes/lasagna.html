Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion (finely diced)
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • ¾ teaspoon Garlic Powder (or 5 garlic cloves, finely minced)
  • ¾ teaspoon Salt
  • ½ teaspoon Pepper
  • 32-40 ounce jar Marinara Sauce (may use more depending on how "saucy" you want it)

Parmesan Ricotta Cheese Filling Ingredients

  • 2 cups Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1 teaspoon Dried Basil
  • ¾ teaspoon Salt
  • ½ teaspoon Pepper
    • >
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 4 cups Mozzarella Cheese (may use more if you want it extra cheesy)

Steps to Make

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and cook for about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add ground beef and increase heat to medium-high.
  5. Cook until the meat begins to brown, about 5 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  7. Lower heat to low and add marinara sauce.
  8. If you want the lasagna to be extra saucy, I suggest adding more sauce.
  9. Let the sauce continue to simmer.

    10. Parmesan Ricotta Cheese Filling Steps

  10. In a small bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, basil, salt, and pepper.
  11. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    12. >
  12. Spread the bottom of a 9 x 13 baking pan with ¼ cup of the meat sauce. Place 3 noodles in a single layers on top of the sauce.
  13. Spread each noodle with parmesan ricotta cheese filling and sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and meat sauce. Repeat.
  14. Finish with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  15. Spray a large piece of foil with non-stick cooking spray and cover the lasagna.
  16. Bake for 15 minutes and then remove the foil. Continue to bake until the cheese if bubbling, about 25 minuteslonger.
